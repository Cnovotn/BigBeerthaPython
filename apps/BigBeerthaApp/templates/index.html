About Beertha

Have you ever shotgunned a cold beverage on the golf course? If you're a golfer, the answer is yes.

Have you ever beerbonged on the course? Chances are, you haven't. Beerbongs have long been considered a serious faux pas on the golf course...until now.

Finally, the first ever beerbong in the shape of a full size golf driver. Simply pour a 12 ounce can into the opening at the foot of the club head, then grab the grip and let it rip. Once refreshed, The Big Beertha stores perfectly in your golf bag. A beerbong for a Gentleman (as well as a proper Lady, of course).

The Big Beertha club head is engineered to easily hold 12 oz, while the shaft is clear acrylic, allowing onlookers to view the impressive flow of liquid, similar to a traditional beerbong. The shaft is threaded, and easily screws into or out of the club head for cleaning purposes.
